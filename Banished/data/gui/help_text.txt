Banished:

Movement: move with the arrow keys.
Attacks:
  A: area of effect; 
     a lot of damage in a medium-sized area;
     takes a lot of stamina.
  S: standard slash attack; 
     moderate damage in the direction you are facing.
  D: dash attack; 
     a lot of damage in a straight line in front of you; 
     takes a medium amount of stamina.
Enemies: Kill all of them!
Inventory:
  I: to toggle visibility.
  Left click: pick up and drop items in slots,
  Right click: equip/use items
Book:
  B: open/close book.
  E/R: turn pages.
  every level a new page will be added for you to read!
Escape: pause the game.
