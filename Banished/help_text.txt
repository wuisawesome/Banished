Banished:
--------- 
Movement: move with the arrow keys.
Attacks:
  A: area of effect; a lot of damage in a medium-sized area; takes a lot of stamina.
  S: standard slash attack; moderate damage in the direction you are facing.
  D: dash attack; a lot of damage in a straight line in front of you; takes a medium amount of stamina.
Enemies: kill all of them!
